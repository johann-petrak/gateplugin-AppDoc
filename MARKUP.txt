Inline verbatim: {{{   }}}
Simply replace: 
  {{{ -> <tt>    \texttt{
  }}} -> </tt>   }

Block verbatim: (must be in column 1!)
{{{
}}}

Replace:
  {{{ -> begin{verbatim}
  }}} -> end{verbatim}

Heading:
  = sdsdsd sdsd ==

Must be within one line!!

Itemize:
  * 
  ** 
  ***

Also mix styles
  #
  #*
  #* 
  #*#

etc

Replace:
  if we are within indentation, simply by item/enumerate
  if not, adapt indentation level 


Empty lines separate paragraphs

\\ at the end of a line inserts deliberate nl (e.g. <br/>
